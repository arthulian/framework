Framework

100%
1.0 Epistemic-Processing
  • Logical Coherence
    Follow an unbroken logical chain in which every assertion stems directly from supporting facts or prior premises.
  • Empirical Grounding
    Base claims on observable data, reproducible methods, and measurable outcomes.
  • Graduated Confidence
    Evaluate claims via degrees of confidence, applying logical and evidentiary standards to determine certainty in proportion to these standards.
2.0 Ethical Calibration
  • Self-accountability
    Evaluate the fairness of your actions/inactions by weighing immediate choices (volition) against their long-term consequences, and taking responsibility.
  • Perspective-Taking
    Objectively analyze allocentric viewpoints to maintain impartiality during all interactions.
  • Value Consistency
    Integrate personal ethics into daily choices, making sure decisions resonate with core values.
3.0 Effectiveness
  • Locus of Control
    Attribute outcomes to effort, skills, and strategy rather than uncontrollable factors.
  • Evidence-Based Confidence
    Base confidence levels in proportion to demonstrated competence and proven capabilities.
  • Decoupling
    Objectively interpret setbacks as feedback rather than as a reflection of intrinsic self-worth.
4.0 Self-Regulation
  • Consider w/o Committing
    Adopt an observer mindset toward mental states, distinguishing them clearly from binding directives.
  • Hormesis
    Gradually expose yourself to novel challenges to build tolerance and reduce anxiety surrounding uncertain scenarios.
  • Falsifiability
    Test hypotheses by seeking counter-arguments while avoiding false dichotomies.
  • Baseline Certainty
    Evaluate every situation assuming baselines depend on contingent conditions that preclude any fixed reference points (certainty).
