Internal State Awareness Notice emotional/cognitive states without identification. Create psychological distance to prevent reactive hijacking.

B. Effort Allocation Matrix

Control Level Resource Strategy Energy Output
✅ Controllable Invest full cognitive & physical resources. Direct action where it changes outcomes.
High / Focused

Moderate / Flexible
