Framework

Humility-Based Honesty

Core Principles

  • Proactively identify falsifiers before acting to address weaknesses.
  • Use consistent evaluation standards, avoiding arbitrary changes.
  • Prioritize "being accurate" over “being right”

Stress-Testing Methods

Mirror Test: Fairly represent opposing viewpoints before judging.

Confidence Calibration: Align belief strength with evidence quality.

Equanimity Regulation

Identity Decoupling

  • Notice internal states without self-identifying with them

Effort Allocation

✅ Controllables

Invest time, energy, and resources where actions affect outcomes.

❌ Uncontrollables

Withhold effort and attention to avoid wasting energy on factors outside your control.

Self-Esteem Anchors

✓ Secure attachment: It anchors self-worth to acting with integrity and being true to oneself.

✗ Anxious attachment: It anchors self-worth to social validation contingent on outcomes.

✗ Avoidant attachment: It anchors self-worth to task-completion and defensive self-reliance.

Adaptive Resilience

Stress Types

  • Distress (Survival mode): Protect the system.
  • The inverted-U Curve (Growth mode): This is the optimal amount of stress that enhances performance and well-being.
