Intellectual & Epistemic Humility

Cognitive Framework
📋

Core Principles

Epistemic Pre-commitment ▼
  • Be aware of your falsifiers before reaching conclusions
  • Commit to your standards to prevent moving goalposts
  • Focus on "getting it right" over "being right"
🎯

Awareness of Limits

Recognize the boundaries of your knowledge and identify the falsifiers already implied by those limits.
🔍

Testing Boundaries

  • Identify weak points and scrutinize reasoning

Leads to Stress-Testing Methods

⚡

Stress-Testing Methods

🪞 Mirror Test

Articulate opposing views fairly and accurately to improve internal clarity

📊 Confidence Calibration
  • Scale belief strength proportionally to evidence quality
  • Avoid binary certainty
🧘

Equanimity Through Self-Mastery

Emotional Regulation
👁️

Observer Mindset

  • Notice internal states without self-identification
  • Create psychological distance to avoid reactive hijacking
⚖️

Effort Allocation

✅ Controllable

Invest full cognitive and physical resources when actions affect outcomes.

⚠️ Partial Control

Scale effort proportionally to influence

❌ Uncontrollable

Refrain from investment to prevent wasted energy

💎

Character-Based Self-Esteem

✓ Anchor self-worth in:
  • Effort & dedication
  • Core values
  • Authenticity
✗ Decouple from:
  • External validation
  • Outcomes & results
  • Others' opinions
